Description

We offer optimum quality Brass Fittings which are used in engineering, mechanical and automobile, automotive and other industrial sectors. Furthermore, the use of quality approved raw material in our range ensures durability, tensile strength and resistivity to adverse conditions. The Range of Brass Fittings are offered in different combinations of sizes, multi-step reductions, and shapes. We manufacture various types of Brass Fittings including Elbow Union, Nut, Sleeve, Male Branch Tee, Male Connector, Female Connector, Brass Sleeve, Hex Socket and many more! Our Range of Brass Fittings is highly suitable to be used in different sorts of industrial applications and appreciated by our global clients.
